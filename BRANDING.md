# TinyMonitor Branding Policy

The TinyMonitor name, logo, visual identity, domain names, and other branding assets are not licensed under the Apache License 2.0 or CC BY 4.0.

## What is allowed

You may use the tinymonitor name to:

- refer to the original tinymonitor project
- describe compatibility with tinymonitor
- link to the official repository
- mention tinymonitor in articles, tutorials, talks, or documentation

Examples:

- "This plugin integrates with tinymonitor."
- "This article explains how tinymonitor works."
- "Forked from tinymonitor."

## What is not allowed

You may not use the tinymonitor name, logo, or branding to:

- present your fork as the official tinymonitor project
- create a confusingly similar product or service
- imply endorsement by the tinymonitor maintainers
- redistribute a modified version under the same branding without clear notice
- use the logo in commercial materials without permission

## Forks and modified versions

Forks are welcome.

If you distribute a modified version, please make it clear that it is a fork or modified distribution.

Recommended wording:

> This project is a fork of TinyMonitor and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the original TinyMonitor maintainers.

## Logo and visual assets

The tinymonitor logo and visual assets may not be reused in modified distributions, commercial products, or confusingly similar projects without permission.

For branding permissions, please contact the project maintainer.
